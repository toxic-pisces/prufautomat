# PWA Installation Instructions for iOS/iPad

## How to Install the App on Your iPad

1. **Open Safari** on your iPad (must use Safari, not Chrome or other browsers)

2. **Navigate** to your app's URL (where you host this application)

3. **Tap the Share button** (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the top of Safari

4. **Scroll down** and tap "Add to Home Screen"

5. **Edit the name** if needed (default is "Machine 03")

6. **Tap "Add"** in the top right corner

7. The app icon will now appear on your home screen

8. **Tap the icon** to launch the app in full-screen mode

## Features

- **Offline Support**: The app will work even without internet connection (cached content)
- **Full Screen**: Runs without Safari's browser UI
- **App Icon**: Beautiful icon on your home screen
- **Fast Access**: Launch instantly like any native app

## Notes

- For the best experience, use landscape orientation on your iPad
- The app will cache files for offline use
- Firebase features require an internet connection
- To update the app, simply refresh it while online

## Troubleshooting

If the "Add to Home Screen" option doesn't appear:
- Make sure you're using Safari (not another browser)
- Check that you're not in Private Browsing mode
- Try reloading the page

If the app doesn't launch properly:
- Delete the app from your home screen
- Clear Safari's cache
- Reinstall following the steps above
